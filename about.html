about me

i'm Annie Sweta Kaur — a builder interested in ai, product, and the messy space between what technology can do and what people actually need from it.

i came to building sideways — through research, conversations with executives, late-night hackathon hacks, and small tools i made for myself that ended up useful to other people. i think the best products start from a real observation about how someone's day actually works.

lately i've been spending time on agentic ai: how autonomous systems can actually help people shop, plan, and decide — without getting in the way. i built kasiey (an ai chief-of-staff) at the anthropic hackathon, and i'm currently researching agentic shopping flows.

outside of work, i interview executives about how ai is showing up in their orgs, build tiny python tools when i'm curious about something (like who stopped following me on instagram), and read a lot.
