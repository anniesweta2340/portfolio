instagram unfollower

a tiny python tool for who stopped following back

rolesolo build
stackpython
statusopen source
linksgithub →

the itch

instagram tells you your follower count but not who's actually drifted away. there's a bunch of sketchy apps that do this — most of them want your password or serve ads over your social graph. i wanted something i could read the source of and run locally.

what it does

  • parses the follower and following json files from instagram's official data export
  • diffs the two lists to show who you follow that doesn't follow back, and vice versa
  • runs entirely locally — no login, no third-party api, no ads

why it's on here

it's small, but it's a good example of how i work: notice a real annoyance, realize the existing solutions are bad for a specific reason, build the smallest thing that fixes it, ship it. useful doesn't have to mean complicated.

what i learned

reading through instagram's data export format was the most interesting part — it's surprisingly rich, and there's more you could build on top of it than what i ended up shipping. a future version might add friendship trajectory over time.
